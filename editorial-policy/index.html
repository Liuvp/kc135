Site Policy

Editorial Policy

kc135.xyz is built as a focused informational site about KC-135-related searches. This page explains how content is sourced, how rumors are handled, and how corrections are made.

Effective date: March 13, 2026 Coverage style: news summary and reference content

Mission

The goal of kc135.xyz is to help readers quickly understand major KC-135 search topics. That includes both breaking-news interest and background questions such as crew, tanker role, cost context, and basic aircraft facts.

Sourcing priorities

The site gives priority to official public references, major wire services, and widely trusted primary-source materials when available. For KC-135 coverage, that usually means official military fact sheets or statements, followed by established news reporting.

Exact dates over vague timing

Breaking stories can become confusing very quickly. To reduce ambiguity, pages on this site use exact dates such as March 12, 2026 or March 13, 2026 instead of relying only on words like today, yesterday, or recently.

Rumors and unconfirmed claims

Search spikes often include rumor-heavy phrases such as shot down, missing, or lost. Those phrases may be discussed only when they are clearly labeled as unconfirmed or when credible reporting addresses them directly. The site does not treat search demand as proof.

Corrections and updates

If a page contains an error or if major new confirmed information becomes public, the page should be updated promptly and the modified date should be revised. Readers can send correction requests through the contact page or by email.

AI-assisted drafting

Some English-language drafts may be prepared with AI assistance to help with structure and writing speed. However, the intended standard for publication is source review and factual checking before pages go live. AI is used as a drafting tool, not as an authority.

Advertising and independence

The site may use advertising, including Google AdSense, to support operations. Advertising does not determine factual conclusions, source selection, or the wording of confirmed and unconfirmed claims.

Corrections

How to report an issue

Email editor@kc135.xyz with the page URL, the issue you found, and a supporting source when possible.
